Homemade Ice Cream Cake

The ultimate dessert for any celebration. This ice cream cake is incredibly versatile, by using any cake mix or ice cream flavor you love. Top it with fudge, Oreos, or sprinkles to make it your own!

Jump to Recipe

Ingredients You Need

  • 1 (15.25 oz) package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ gallon chocolate ice cream

Step By Step

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

2. Mix cake mix, water, eggs, and oil. Beat until smooth and pour into dish.

3. Bake for 26-30 mins until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.

4. Slice the ice cream block in half lengthwise using string or floss.

5. Place cooled cake over ice cream slabs and trim edges so they are flush.

6. Flip the cake onto a platter, remove waxed paper, and smooth the seam.

7. Cover with waxed paper and freeze until firm. Decorate and serve!
