Easy Indian Butter Chicken

A perfected recipe for creamy, restaurant-style butter chicken. The sauce is the star here, which perfectly balanced spices and a velvety texture that pairs beautifully with naan or basmati rice.

Jump to Recipe

Ingredients You Need

  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 ½ lbs chicken breast (bite-sized chunks)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala

Step By Step

1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Melt butter and caramelize onion and garlic until dark brown (15 mins).

3. Combine cream, tomato sauce, spices, and simmer in a saucepan.

4. Simmer for 30 mins, stirring occasionally, then stir in onions.

5. Toss chicken in oil and tandoori masala; spread on a baking sheet.

6. Bake chicken for 12 minutes until cooked through (no longer pink).

7. Combine chicken with sauce, serve, and enjoy!
