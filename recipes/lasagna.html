How To Make Lasagna

This is a classic, hearty lasagna recipe featuring layers of savory beef sauce, a creamy cheese blend, and perfectly tender noodles. Forget the butter chicken—this is the ultimate comfort food!

Jump to Recipe

Ingredients You Need

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 ounces) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Step By Step

1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. Brown beef in a skillet for 8-10 mins. Drain grease and stir in sauce.

3. Combine cottage cheese, mozzarella, eggs, and spices in a large bowl.

4. Layer noodles, cheese mixture, and sauce. Pour water along edges and cover.

5. Bake for 45 mins covered, then 10 mins uncovered. Let stand for 10 mins.

6. Serve and enjoy your delicious masterpiece!
